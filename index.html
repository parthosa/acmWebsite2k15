About Us

We are the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), Student Chapter, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani or in short, BITSacm. The student chapter in BITS Pilani was established in 2008, but in two years, we have grown into the largest student chapter in the world with over 800 Chapter Members and 50 Council members. Both groups consist of students from all subjects and of all years – freshmen to seniors.

Expansion

This year, along with the entry of 30 new Council Members, we are projecting an increase of about 300 Chapter Members after the new recruitment session, to be held shortly.

Goal

We aim to instill the spirit of technology and computing among the students of BITS, through the various fields modern Information Technology has developed in.
